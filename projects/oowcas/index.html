Problem

Many students lack access to diverse extracurricular activities and a collaborative student forum, limiting their opportunities to develop skills, expand knowledge, and build meaningful connections.

In today's fast-paced educational landscape, students often find themselves confined to the rigid boundaries of traditional classrooms. They yearn for a platform where they can break free from these constraints, explore their interests, and interact with peers who share their passions.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to thrive academically and personally. Our platform is designed to break down barriers, provide access to a plethora of extracurricular activities, and facilitate meaningful interactions among students.

Features

Clubs

Variety of clubs that offer an opportunity for students to connect, explore, and have fun. Wide range of interests, from sports and arts to academics and science. Great opportunities to explore different activities and interests, while also having fun.

Forum

A great place to get peer advice and feedback. A safe and inclusive space to discuss any academic, social, or career-related topics. Resources and support to help students reach their full potential.

Research

Our journey began with extensive research into the challenges students face in today's educational landscape. We conducted in-depth surveys, interviews, and focus groups with students from diverse backgrounds to understand their needs and pain points.

This research revealed a consistent theme - the lack of access to extracurricular activities and a supportive student community. Students expressed a strong desire for a platform that could provide opportunities to learn beyond the classroom and connect with peers who shared their interests.

Opportunity

Armed with this knowledge, we saw a unique opportunity to bridge this gap and create something truly transformative. By addressing the needs identified in our research, we believed we could empower students to excel academically and personally.

Our platform would not only provide a space for students to access a diverse range of extracurricular activities but also encourage collaboration and knowledge-sharing, thereby nurturing holistic development.

Exploration

The exploration phase involved the development of OOWCAS as a versatile platform. We worked tirelessly to design and build features that would meet the diverse needs of students.

Our team collaborated with educators, technologists, and designers to create a user-friendly interface that fosters inclusivity, encourages innovation, and promotes diversity. Through iterative testing and feedback loops, we fine-tuned the platform to ensure it aligned perfectly with our vision.

Infrastructure had to be dynamic, so we built the foundation of the application to retrieve data from a WIX Server to simplify the login, forum and club systems
